## Mind Gate Origin Card

\( G_{mind} = 10 \times X_{insp} + Y_{reserve} + 1 / J_{life} \)

- X → life (inspiration reservoir)
- Y → recovery (glyph-fed renewal)
- Z → resonance (sigil cohesion)

Tune X to deepen the Mind Gate's stores as tides shift through inspiration.
Amplify Y so recovery surges the gate between waves.

**Base Cost:** 10 þ
