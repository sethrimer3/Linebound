# World & Level Outlines

This document captures the reorganized eight-world campaign structure. Each world lists its four stages (with the fourth entry serving as the area's boss confrontation) and a short outline describing the level's identity, environmental hooks, and enemy focus.

## World 1 – Verdant Marches
- **1-1 Meadow March** – Training green with rolling berms and raider skirmishers that teach basic movement and melee spacing.
- **1-2 Silvergrove Crossing** – Forested bridges over a slow river, introducing vertical platforms, archer crossfire, and breakable cover.
- **1-3 Thicketway Garrison** – Dense woods threaded with barricades and rope lifts where mages and rogues coordinate ambushes.
- **1-4 Verdant Skyways (Boss)** – Suspended gardens and windlifts above canyon gaps culminating in the airship sentry duel.

## World 2 – Frostwind Range
- **2-1 Frostwind Ridge** – Stepped ice ledges and chasms with skippers and sentries pushing players to manage slippery footing.
- **2-2 Galecrust Outpost** – Blizzard-swept fortlet featuring rotating windgates, frozen ballistae, and shield-bearing raiders.
- **2-3 Shatterglass Caverns** – Crystalline caves with breakaway floors, reflecting beams, and mage formations guarding heat vents.
- **2-4 Hoarfrost Bastion (Boss)** – Siege atop an ice bastion where the Glacier Warden deploys spear charges and falling icicles.

## World 3 – Cinder Depths
- **3-1 Cinder Cavern** – Molten galleries with collapsing platforms, ember cultists, and lavafalls that shape combat lanes.
- **3-2 Emberwind Crucible** – Forge halls powered by bellows drafts, mixing boomlings with flame jets and rotating kiln doors.
- **3-3 Magma Conflux** – Bridge network suspended above lava rivers with timed eruptions and artillery cultists forcing movement.
- **3-4 Ashen Throne (Boss)** – Final duel atop a magma throne where the Cinder Sovereign combines bomb barrages and flame walls.

## World 4 – Abyssal Dominion
- **4-1 Abyssal Engineworks** – Coral-encrusted foundry floors balancing water lifts, steam vents, and anchor bruisers.
- **4-2 Tidal Wells** – Flood chambers alternating between drained and submerged states while tide wardens reshape terrain.
- **4-3 Twilight Trench** – Deep-sea trench lit by bioluminescent pylons, featuring vent arcanists and current-swept arenas.
- **4-4 Abyssal Crown (Boss)** – Leviathan encounter amidst whirlpool platforms and crushing pressure pulses.

## World 5 – Sunscorched Dominion
- **5-1 Inspire Dunes** – Wind-carved dunes and sandstone sentries with rolling hazards and shifting sandfalls.
- **5-2 Mirage Bazaar** – Desert market ruins hiding ambush alleys, mirage illusions, and sandglass traps that delay foes.
- **5-3 Sirocco Causeway** – Elevated caravan bridges battered by sandstorms where rollers and windthrowers attack from range.
- **5-4 Auric Apex Citadel (Boss)** – Gilded storm-fortress culminating in the sovereign duel amid lightning-charged ramparts.

## World 6 – Umbral Reliquary
- **6-1 Umbral Reliquary** – Dark reliquary corridors lit by braziers, emphasizing torchlight stealth and shadow ambushes.
- **6-2 Gloomfall Colonnade** – Crumbling hall with cascading voidfalls and phasing sentries shifting between columns.
- **6-3 Obsidian Warrens** – Labyrinthine basalt tunnels patrolled by wraith blades and warding glyph nodes.
- **6-4 Midnight Throne (Boss)** – Duel in the heart reliquary where the warden bends darkness into sweeping blades.

## World 7 – Chronometric Spires
- **7-1 Chronometric Symptom** – Clockwork plaza where pendulum hazards telegraph the world's time-manipulation mechanics.
- **7-2 Pendulum Engine** – Gear-lined corridors with swinging weights, time dilation fields, and springing clockwork foes.
- **7-3 Gear Spire Ascent** – Vertical climb through exposed gearshafts, rotating elevators, and ranged custodians.
- **7-4 Clocktower Crown (Boss)** – Apex duel against the timekeeper wielding chronal rewind bursts and gear storms.

## World 8 – Liminal Expanse
- **8-1 Space Between Time** – Quiet starlit hallway surrounding a central pyramid guarded by glyph specters.
- **8-2 Spiral of the Lost Stars** – Fractured causeways orbiting a void chasm with rotating constellations dictating safe paths.
- **8-3 Liminal Guile** – Platform labyrinth drifting between dimensions with phasing foes and shifting gravity gates.
- **8-4 Parallax Model (Boss)** – Final confrontation across overlapping arenas where reality layers rotate mid-fight.

## Optional Encounters
- **Neon Citadel (Set Extra)** – Standalone duel against the Radiant Warden that unlocks once the early canopy arc is complete.
- **Chronoglass Expanse (Set Extra)** – Mirrored dune gauntlet tucked into the optional time-bent branch for post-campaign mastery runs.
